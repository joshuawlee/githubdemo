Importance

My digital portfolio is important to me as it can serve as a timeline that displays my progress and accomplishments I will achieve along my computer science journey. This timeline will display my knowledge and competence through the classes I have taken, projects I have completed, and even research I have conducted. This page of development will allow me to better captivate my future job interviews or even college admissions by displaying my skills beyond my GPA and classes I have taken.
Furthermore, this digital portfolio serves as a great learning tool as it allows me to delve into challenging projects and topics to better develop my computer science knowledge. Additionally, the portfolio serves as an easy way to access all of my work, so I can go back and refresh my memory on certain topics, or reflect upon how my skills have improved.
